Clear chicken stew

We will be making my wife's clear chicken stew, a delicious meal.

What do you need?

  • 1l of water
  • Knorr base for chicken stew
  • 500g of chicken fillet
  • 30g of vermicelli pasta
  • 4 cubes of broth base

How do you make it?

  1. Cut the chicken into small pieces
  2. Put every ingredient in a big pot
  3. Stir well and put the stove on low heat once boiling starts
  4. Let simmer for a few hours
  5. Enjoy!
