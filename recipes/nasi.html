Nasi Goreng

I will teach you how to make delicious Nasi Goreng!

What do you need?

  • 1 cucumber
  • 150ml vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 300g pandan rice
  • 450ml of water
  • 2 sjalots
  • 3 red peppers
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 5 tablespoons of sunflower oil
  • 6 eggs
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons of ketjap manis
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of ketjap asin
  • 2 teaspoons of shrimp paste

That looks good even when not combined! How do we do it?

  1. Make thin slices of cucumber. Heat the vinegar with the sugar and half of the salt in a pan until the sugar and salt have dissolved, this should take 3 minutes. Take it off the stove, put the cucumber slices in the vinegar and leave for 1 hour. Shuffle after 30 minutes.
  2. Wash the rice 3-4 times in water, until the water is less murky. Put the rice in a pot and fill with water. Start boiling and let all the water evaporate on medium heat. Put a lid on the pot. Leave for 1 hour.
  3. Cut the sjalots. Cut the peppers. Peel the garlic. Mash everything together with a pestle and mortar (boemboe). Heat 2/5 of the oil in a wok pan on medium heat. Bake the boemboe for at least 5 minutes while keeping to shuffle. Put everyhting on one side of the wok pan.
  4. Put 1 egg in the wok pan and bake it. Add the rice and bake everything for 3 minutes on high heat, until all ingredients have mixed well. Make everything taste good by adding the Ketjap manis and asin. Put in the shrimp paste. Meanwhile cook the 2 other eggs in a skillet.
  5. Serve the Nasi Goreng with the two eggs on top, delicious if eaten with kroepoek, enjoy!
