Delicious Boerenkool

In this recipe i will teach you how to make Boerenkool.

To make this beautiful Dutch dish you don't need a lot of ingredients only a big pot and some heavy lifting are required for this all to work out.

What do you need?

  • 1kg Boerenkool
  • 1kg Potatoes
  • 2 Rookworsten (preferably HEMA worsten)
  • 500g Bacon bits
  • Pepper to your liking

Okay, so how do we make it?

  1. peel all the potatoes and throw them in the pot.
  2. Pour water in the pot until it reaches the potato level.
  3. Take the raw boerenkool and put that on top.
  4. On top of that put the rookworst and start boiling it all.
  5. While that's cooking take a skillet and bake the bacon bits
  6. When everything is done mash the boerenkool and the potatoes together with the bacon bits and put it all on plates.
  7. Cut the rookworst, make sure everyone has a piece and enjoy!
